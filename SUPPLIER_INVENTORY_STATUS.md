# Supplier-Side Industrial Inventory Features

## ✅ IMPLEMENTED - Supplier Inventory Enhanced

I've updated the **Supplier Inventory** page to use the same comprehensive industrial-grade vehicle detail modal as dealerships!

### What Suppliers Now Have:

When suppliers click on any vehicle in their inventory, they see the **SAME comprehensive detail modal** with:

#### 📋 **12 Complete Information Sections:**
1. **Vehicle Specifications** - All technical details
2. **Delivery & Timing** - Delivery dates, days in inventory
3. **Location & Storage** - Warehouse, parking bay
4. **Documentation & Compliance** - Import permits, registration status
5. **Insurance** - Policy details, expiry dates
6. **Condition & Inspection** - Inspection scores, condition ratings
7. **Service & Maintenance** - Service history, repairs
8. **Financial Details** - Complete cost breakdown (FOB, shipping, duties, taxes)
9. **Pricing Strategy** - Min/target prices, profit margins
10. **Marketing & Sales** - Listing status, views, inquiries
11. **Shipping Information** - Container, BL, vessel details
12. **Notes** - Public and internal notes
